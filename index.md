Hello, I'm Jason. I'm a Data Scientist at ESR (Institute of Environmental Science and Research). I enjoy learning and researching machine learning methods to solve problems -- with coding in python, R.  

My interests includes:
  - Computer Vision 
    - Image Classification (Firearm evidence identification), 
    - Metric learning (Tablet image matching), 
    - Segmentation -- I fine-tunned SAM for ruler segmentation to develop an [algorithm](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1355030624000728) for image auto-scalling.
    - Image comparison -- I developed a [CNN-based method](https://www.esr.cri.nz/digital-library/shoeprint-image-retrieval-and-crime-scene-shoeprint-image-linking-by-using-convolutional-neural-network-and-normalized-cross-correlation/) for shoeprint image retrieval.
  
  - Statistical modelling -- I developed a [statistical model](https://www.esr.cri.nz/digital-library/bayesian-mixture-modelling-for-glass-refractive-index-measurement/) to assist the interpretation of forensic glass evidence.
  
  - R Shiny -- Developing ShinyApp for interpretation of glass evidence, illistic tablet matching
  
  - Generative AI -- Fine-tunning stable diffusion model (xd, xdsl, turbo) for domain adoptation (draw anime character!), Fine-tunning LLM (LLaMa) to adopt forensic knowledge used internally at ESR.

  - WarcraftIII RPG map maker -- I'm a fan of RPG in WarcraftIII. I maked maps called [City of water -- 水之都](https://dl.3dmgame.com/patch/154945.html) and [City of wind -- 风之都](https://www.ou99.com/portal.php?aid=41311&mod=view&page=1). Please have a play if you are interested in! 😊
