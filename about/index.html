About

Hello 你好 雷猴~, I'm Zhijian (Jason). I'm a Data Scientist at ESR (Institute of Environmental Science and Research). I enjoy learning and researching machine learning methods to solve problems -- with coding in python, R.

My interests includes:

  • Computer Vision

    • Image Classification (Firearm evidence identification)

    • Metric learning (Tablet image matching),

    • Segmentation -- I fine-tuned SAM for ruler segmentation to develop an algorithm for image auto-scaling.

    • Image comparison -- I developed a CNN-based method for shoeprint image retrieval.

  • Statistical modelling -- I developed a statistical model to assist the interpretation of forensic glass evidence.

  • R Shiny -- Developing ShinyApp for interpretation of glass evidence, illistic tablet matching

  • Generative AI -- Fine-tuning stable diffusion model (xd, xdsl, turbo) for domain adaptation (draw anime character!), Fine-tuning LLM (LLaMa) to adopt forensic knowledge used internally at ESR.

  • WarcraftIII RPG map maker -- I'm a fan of RPG in WarcraftIII. I made maps called City of water -- 水之都 and City of wind -- 风之都. Please have a play if you are interested in! 😊
